How to Connect Google Sheet
Step 1. Open your Google Sheet with the trade data
Step 2. Go to File → Share → Publish to web
Step 3. Select each sheet tab, choose CSV format, click Publish
Step 4. Copy the Sheet ID from the URL bar — it looks like:
docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/THIS-PART/edit
Step 5. Paste that ID above and click Save & Connect
The sheet must have 3 tabs named exactly: Import, Export, Bank Guarantee
